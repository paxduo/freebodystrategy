How It Works

1. Identify Forces

Recognize external forces (market trends, competition) and interior forces (resources, culture) affecting your business.

2. Assign Intensities

Specify the intensity of each force on a scale of 1–10. The strength of each force will contribute to your overall strategy.

3. Calculate Net Force

Process your forces to compute a net directional “force” – inspired by Newton’s second law – and earn points along the way.
